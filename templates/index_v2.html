📖 Project Documentation

1. System Overview

This AI Trading Bot is an advanced automated system that intelligently manages your portfolio on the Alpaca platform. It combines market data analysis, news sentiment tracking, and Artificial Intelligence to make informed decisions for you.

2. Trading Modes

  • Swing Trading: Focuses on longer-term trends, holding positions for days or weeks to capture major moves.
  • Day Trading: Operates at high speed during market hours, buying and selling within the same day to profit from short-term volatility.

3. The Decision Process

Step 1: Risk Check

First, it ensures your portfolio is healthy and within safe risk limits. Safety is the #1 priority.

Step 2: Portfolio Check

It reviews what you currently own. Is it time to take profit? Should we cut a loss?

Step 3: Market Scan

Scans market for Momentum, Growth, and Value opportunities.

Step 4: AI Analysis

Reads news, analyzes trends, and decides Buy/Sell/Hold.

Step 5: Execution

Executes trades instantly on your broker account.

Core Strategies

1. Straddles

A neutral strategy where you buy both a call and a put option with the same strike price and expiration date.

Best for: High volatility when you expect a big move but don't know the direction.

2. Butterflies

A limited-risk, limited-profit strategy using multiple option contracts with the same expiration but different strike prices.

Best for: Low volatility when you expect the price to stay within a specific range.

3. Spreads

Buying and selling options of the same class (calls or puts) on the same underlying security with different strike prices or expirations.

Best for: Directional bets with defined risk and reward.
