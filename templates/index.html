1. System Overview

This AI Trading Bot is an advanced automated system that intelligently manages your portfolio on the Alpaca platform. It combines market data analysis, news sentiment tracking, and Artificial Intelligence to make informed decisions for you.

2. Trading Modes

  • Swing Trading: Focuses on longer-term trends, holding positions for days or weeks to capture major moves.
  • Day Trading: Operates at high speed during market hours, buying and selling within the same day to profit from short-term volatility.

3. The Decision Process

The bot continuously cycles through a smart decision-making process:

Step 1: Risk Check
First, it ensures your portfolio is healthy and within safe risk limits. Safety is the #1 priority.
Step 2: Portfolio Check
It reviews what you currently own. Is it time to take profit? Should we cut a loss?
Step 3: Market Scan
The bot scans the entire market for opportunities, looking for:
  • Momentum: Stocks moving fast with high volume.
  • Growth: Companies with strong financial expansion.
  • Value: Good companies currently undervalued.
Step 4: AI Brain Analysis
This is the "Brain". It reads the latest news, analyzes market trends, and reviews the financial data of potential candidates. It then decides whether to Buy, Sell, or Hold, providing a reason for every decision.
Step 5: Execution
Once a decision is validated for safety, the bot executes the trade instantly on your broker account.

Core Strategies

1. Straddles

A neutral strategy where you buy both a call and a put option with the same strike price and expiration date. Best for: High volatility when you expect a big move but don't know the direction.

2. Butterflies

A limited-risk, limited-profit strategy using multiple option contracts with the same expiration but different strike prices. Best for: Low volatility when you expect the price to stay within a specific range.

3. Spreads

Buying and selling options of the same class (calls or puts) on the same underlying security with different strike prices or expirations. Best for: Directional bets with defined risk and reward (e.g., Bull Call Spread, Bear Put Spread).
